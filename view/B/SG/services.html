Services

Delivery Service

Parcel delivery is $15

Order up to a weight of 40kg, and a maximum length per item of 1.8 meters.

  • Parcel size: (L)59cm x (W)34cm x (H)39cm

Delivery will take place within 3-5 working days.
Parcel delivery time: 9am - 6pm.

Truck delivery is $35

Delivery typically takes place within 2-5 days from the date of order.

  • Please note for truck delivery, an additional flat rate of $20 will apply if there is no lift access, or if items cannot fit in the lift at the delivery address located in a building from the 3rd floor onwards. This service is applicable up to the 5th floor only. Please pay the fee directly to the service provider.
  • Please note that drilling services are only available with assembly purchases. Delivery-only purchases are not applicable for drilling services.
  • Please note that during major holiday seasons*, please allow an extra 1-3 business days for processing time on truck and parcel delivery orders due to the high peak of orders.
    *Chinese New Year, Hari Raya Puasa, Deepavali, Christmas, etc.
Assembly Service

Service Fees

1. All other furniture that requires assembly: 20% of the original product's retail price.

2. Sofa and Sofa bed assembly: 10% of the original product's retail price.

3. A $5 charge per drilled hole applies to products, which require to be wall-mounted.

4. Drilling services are only available with assembly purchases.

5. Integrated light assembly will only be available with purchases of PAX wardrobe assembly services in stores.

* Electric and plumbing work is not included in assembly services. Actual drilling costs depend on the situation at your premises.
